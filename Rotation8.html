Rotation 8 - Cameron Burford

Soldering

Gowing up my parents used to own a hobby store.

That being said I always grew up around electronics, soldering, and tinkering.

As weird as it is to say, this rotation really sent me back to my childhood.

Soldering came back to me relatively easily and was just a matter of plugging and chugging throughout the assignments.

The first project we were to make was a strobe light.

This proved to be a easy project and was completed relatively quickly.
