Rotation 3 - Cameron Burford

Garment and Sublimation Printing

This third rotation of classes was really the first time we were able to make something within the class.

The first item we were instructed to make was a t-shirt that can connect the local Hudson OH, area or WRA in some way or form.

Naturally not being a creative person, I decided to connect the WRA baseball team and Ohio \ by inlaying a baseball within the state.

An additional item that was to be created this rotation was something that was sublimated.

Sublimation is the process of printing an image on to a special piece of paper, as well as a specific type of ink.

During this process, heat is used to transfer the image on to things such as coasters, and mugs..

I decided to make two coasters for my father, both of his Ferrari 348 TS.

He claims to enjoy both coasters, and uses them daily.
