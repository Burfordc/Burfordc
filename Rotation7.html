Rotation 7 - Cameron Burford

Vacumm Forming and Lamination

This process of Vacumm forming was easily one of the most physically demanding processes that we did throughout the year.

Although hysically demanding it was a rather easy process.

The sheets of maple that were laid out were coated in thin layers of clue and then placed on top of a mold within a vacumm bag.

When the vacumm was pulled the pressure forced the wood around the form and made the wood take the shape of a board.

I kepth the design of the board relatively simple and heres why:

I intend for this board to be used as a display piece for one of my cars sponsors, So Scare.

This board will be covered in the stickers they produce and will be used to display them at local car meets in Calgary.

The boards top has been left untouched in order to provide the most surface area for advertising.
