Rotation 4 - Cameron Burford

Laser cutting and Laser Engraving

During this rotation we were tasked with producing 3 things with the laser cutter.

The first item I produced was the Drift Dialects Clock

I am a fan of the Drift Dialects company and I even contacted the owner.

The clock will be displayed in their show room and I will be receiving some goods from them in return.

This rotation we were also tasked with creating two other objects.

The first item I chose to do was a Ferrari glass. This glass was done with the rotarty tool within the laser cutter.

The rotary tool and the raster ability of the laser allowed the glass to be engraved.
